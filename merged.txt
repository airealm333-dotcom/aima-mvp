EXPLANATORY NOTE TO THE RESPONDENT
1. The Claimant has filed a Claim/Counterclaim (referred to as "Claim" for ease of reference)
against you, the Respondent.
2. You must login to the Community Justice and Tribunal System (CJTS) and access the case
using the One-Time-Password (OTP) provided on the Notice of Case Management
Conference. If you encounter problems, please contact the State Courts at 6587 8423.
3. If you intend to file a response to the Claim, you must file and serve your Response (and
supporting documents) within 7 days after the date on which you received the Claim. Your
Response may be filed via the Community Justice and Tribunals System ("CJTS”) as
follows:
i. Click on the "Enter one-time reference number" on the top-right corner of your CJTS
"Home" page.
ii. Enter the Case Number and the one-time reference number which can be found on the
Notice of Case Management Conference served on you by the Claimant.
iii. Once you can see the Case Number under "Active Case(s)", click on "Response" under
"Remarks/Action"; and
iv. Fill up the Response form accordingly.
v. And upload a copy of all supporting evidence, including documents, photographs, video
recordings, audio recording, electronic evidence etc. to prove the matters stated in your
Response. These should be uploaded to the Community Justice and Tribunals System
("CJTS") via “Online Application - Submit Supporting Documents". For video and audio
recordings, you must provide them in CD-ROM together with a transcript of the contents.
You must provide the English translation of any evidence that is not in the English
language.
4. At the same time, you must serve the Response on the Claimant within 7 days after the date
on which you received the claim through one of the following methods:
a. by leaving a copy of the Response with the Claimant; or
b. by sending a copy of the Response by registered post to the proper address of the
Claimant.
5. You must file a 'Declaration of Service' in CJTS within 4 weeks after the date on which the
Response is filed or before the next case management conference, whichever is earlier.
6. You must attend the Case Management Conference on the date, time and place which can be
found on the Notice of Case Management Conference.
7. If you are a sole proprietor, you must attend the Case Management Conference. If you are a
partnership, a partner or a full-time employee may attend the Case Management Conference.
If you are a company, an officer or a full-time employee may attend the Case Management
Conference. The company's representative must upload a letter of authorization in the
company's letterhead and signed by the director or partner named in the ACRA, via "Online
Application - Submit Supporting Documents" before attending the Case Management
Conference.
8. Where the Case Management Conference is over videoconference, you should do the
following on the scheduled date:
a. Have your Response form and supporting documents ready.
b. Log-on to the Zoom meeting using the Meeting ID and Password sent to you by the
Registry via CJTS Correspondence; and
C. Ensure that you are logged on 15 minutes before the appointed time.
